Cinnamon Toast Recipe

Description

To complete the morning with hot tea, here's the simple cinnamon toast recipe, make sure to use lots of butter!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons of butter (or more!)
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of true cinnamon

Steps

  1. Use a toaster to toast the bread to desired darkness.
  2. Spread butter onto each side of each bread slice.
  3. In a cup or small bowl, stir together the sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle generously over hot buttered toast.
